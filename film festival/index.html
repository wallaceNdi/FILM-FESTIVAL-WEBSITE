Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival

Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival is a yearly organize film festival in the Brooklyn Park in New York with a capacity of about 5,000 people.Jennifer Viala the founder of Brooklyn Vibes Events Co. is the organizer of this year event which begin in August 5th-August 8th 2019.
